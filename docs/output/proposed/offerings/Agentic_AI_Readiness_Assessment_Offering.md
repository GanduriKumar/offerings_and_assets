# [Brand Placeholder] Enterprise Agentic AI Readiness Assessment

## The Offer
**A fixed-fee, senior-led assessment that helps enterprise leaders decide where agentic AI is credible, what the organization is ready to pilot now, and what must be controlled before moving forward.**

This is a decision product for organizations exploring agents, assistants, workflow automation, tool use, and human-in-the-loop execution. It is designed for sponsors who want clarity before committing budget, exposing enterprise systems, or creating operational risk.

## Who This Is For
- CIOs, CTOs, COOs, and digital leaders evaluating enterprise AI moves
- GCC leaders and transformation heads shaping productivity and operating-model bets
- delivery and operations leaders assessing workflow automation beyond basic copilots
- executive sponsors who need a credible view before approving a pilot

## The Buyer Problem
Most organizations do not need more agentic AI ideas. They need a way to separate credible opportunities from expensive noise.

The immediate executive questions are simple:

- Which workflows are actually suitable for agentic AI?
- What is ready to pilot now, not in theory?
- Where are the governance, observability, and failure-containment gaps?
- Should leadership proceed, narrow scope, defer, or stop?

## The Promise
**In two weeks, leadership gets a clear recommendation on where agentic AI can create value, which workflows are fit for pilot, what conditions are missing, and what the next move should be.**

## What The Client Receives

### 1. Executive Decision Summary
A concise leadership-ready summary covering:

- where agentic AI is credible
- where it is premature or risky
- what should be piloted next
- what should be deferred or rejected

### 2. Workflow Suitability Review
A practical view of 2 to 4 target workflows against criteria such as:

- multi-step workflow fit
- tool and system dependency
- exception handling complexity
- human oversight needs
- measurable business outcome potential

### 3. Readiness and Control View
A focused view of the operating conditions required to move safely:

- governance gaps
- failure containment needs
- observability and evaluation readiness
- ownership and operating-model readiness

### 4. Go / No-Go Recommendation
A direct recommendation for each shortlisted workflow:

- proceed to pilot
- proceed with narrowed scope
- defer until key gaps are closed
- do not pursue

### 5. 90-Day Next-Step Path
A practical next-step plan covering the recommended immediate move:

- pilot design
- governance shaping
- executive working session
- advisory support
